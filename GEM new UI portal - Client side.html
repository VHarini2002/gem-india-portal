How do I approve findings from my engine inspection?
Go to the engine detail page, open the Findings tab, and click Approve or Request Review. Your CAM is notified immediately.
How long does a typical teardown take?
Most teardowns run 6–14 weeks depending on engine type and findings. Your live timeline is on the engine detail page.
What happens when parts need to be replaced?
Your CAM raises a PO confirmation request in the portal. You will get a banner notification to approve or query it.
Can I download reports for my records?
Yes. Inspection reports, findings logs, and cost summaries are downloadable anytime from the Files section.
Who do I contact if something is urgent?
Use Call Now above to reach your CAM directly. After-hours on-call details are shown on your CAM card.
